About Me

I am an applied mathematician specialized in numerical analysis of partial differential equations, scientific computing, and machine learning. I received my PhD in Mathematics from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville where I worked with Dr. Cory Hauck on computational methods for transport equations.

My research is motivated by the increasing need for efficient and accurate numerical methods for solving partial differential equations.

In my free time, I enjoy writing music for bass guitar and piano, exercising, cooking, and playing chess.
