About Me

I am an applied mathematician specializing in numerical analysis of partial differential equations, scientific computing, and machine learning. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, where I worked with Dr. Cory Hauck on computational methods for transport equations.

My research is motivated by the increasing need for efficient and accurate numerical methods for solving complex partial differential equations.

In my free time, I enjoy writing music for bass guitar and piano, exercising, cooking, and playing chess.
